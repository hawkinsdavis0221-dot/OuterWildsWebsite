Planets

The solar system is full of strange and wonderful places. Each planet has its own mysteries waiting to be uncovered.

  • Timber Hearth- A quiet homeworld filled with forests, geysers, and familiar beginings
  • Attlerock- A small, rocky moon marked by craters and early discoveries
  • Ash Twin- A hollow twin filled with ancient machinery and hidden purpose
  • Ember Twin- A dessert world slowy buried by rising sands
  • Brittle Hollow- A collapsing world of ruins slowy falling into a black hole
  • Hollows Lantern- A volcanic moon that rains fire down on it's fractured planet
  • Giant's Deep- An ocean planet of violent storms hiding secrets beneath it's surface
  • Orbital Probe Cannon- A shattered station endlessey launching probes into space
  • Dark Bramble- A tangled, infinite maze where space doesn't behave as it should.
  • Quantum Moon- A shifting moon that moves when unobserved
  • The Interloper- A wandering comet with a cold, enigmatic presence
  • Sun Station- A silent station orbiting close to the sun, its purpose long abandoned
